about

Our research interests are in biorefinery and biological systems for wastes and wastewater treatment and production of new generation energy and chemicals.We are also efficiently working on functionalization and utilization of the key building-block chemicals from lignocellulosic biomass, underscoring the utility of both classical and newly developed methods in lignocellulosic biorefinery.
