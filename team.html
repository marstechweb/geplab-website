Group 1

Head Of Marketing

Jerry obtained his MSc Degree from the CEE Department at the Hong Kong PolyU. Jerry is currently in charge of a R&D project “Pilot study on efficiencies of aeration systems using off-gas method at secondary sewage treatment works (STWs)” funded by the Drainage Service Department (DSD). His research focuses on elucidating the gas transfer theory and the related kinetics modeling. He has constructed two real-time off-gas monitoring systems which aim to determine aeration efficiency and biological activities in aerobic/anaerobic systems.
